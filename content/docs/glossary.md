# Glossary
## Hapkido

## Jujitsu
tai sabaki
: body management, mainly footwork to get you out of the line of attack and into a position to counter.

push stepping
: using your back foot to propel you forward (or whatever direction you need to go in).

mokuso 
: clearing the mind.
